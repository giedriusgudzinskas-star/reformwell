You can take payments and deliver files with NO server and NO coding using either Gumroad (simplest, recommended to start) or Stripe Payment Links (lowest fees, more control). OPTION A — GUMROAD (easiest, file delivery built in): 1) Go to gumroad.com and create a free account; complete the payout settings by adding your bank account or PayPal so you actually get paid. 2) Click 'New product' > 'Digital product'. 3) Upload your program PDF and any extras (tracker, quick-start guide) directly to the product — Gumroad stores the files and delivers them automatically after purchase. 4) Set the name, description, cover image, and price (e.g. 39 / 89 / 149 USD). 5) Add a 'Receipt note' or content section with a thank-you and a reminder to read the medical disclaimer. 6) Publish, then copy the product's share link. 7) Put that link behind every 'Buy' button on your site. After payment, Gumroad emails the buyer a secure download link automatically — you do nothing manually. OPTION B — STRIPE PAYMENT LINKS (lower fees; you handle delivery): 1) Create an account at stripe.com and complete identity/bank verification so you can receive payouts. 2) In the Stripe Dashboard go to Product catalog > Add product, enter the name and price for each program. 3) Go to Payment Links > Create payment link, select that product. 4) Under 'After payment', choose 'Show confirmation page' or, better, 'Redirect customers to your website' and point it at a private 'thank-you' page on your site that contains the download links (use an unguessable URL like yoursite.com/dl/x9f2k-back-reset). Alternatively, enable Stripe's 'Send a receipt' and paste the download link into the post-purchase email/confirmation text. 5) Save and copy the Payment Link URL; attach it to your 'Buy' buttons. 6) For file hosting, upload your PDFs to a service like Dropbox, Google Drive (set 'Anyone with the link'), or your site, and use those links on the thank-you page. PRACTICAL ADVICE: Start with Gumroad because automatic file delivery removes the biggest risk for a non-technical founder — buyers always get their file even while you sleep. Move high-volume best-sellers to Stripe later to save on fees. Whichever you choose, test it by buying your own product once (use a real card, then refund yourself in the dashboard) to confirm the buyer receives the file. Add a short line near checkout: 'Secure checkout via Stripe/Gumroad. Your program is delivered instantly by email.' Keep your disclaimer visible before the buy button so customers agree to it before paying.
